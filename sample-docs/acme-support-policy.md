# Acme Gadgets — Customer Support Policy

## Refunds and Returns

At Acme Gadgets we want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund to your original payment method. To qualify for a refund, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase such as an order number or receipt. Refunds are normally processed within 5 to 7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Please note that original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is the result of our error, such as a defective product or an incorrect item being shipped.

Certain products cannot be returned for hygiene or safety reasons. These include opened earphones, personal grooming devices, and any item marked as final sale at the time of purchase. Gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash except where required by law. If you received a damaged or defective item, contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery and we will arrange a free replacement or refund, including return shipping costs.

## Shipping

Acme Gadgets offers several shipping options at checkout. Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days within the continental United States and is free on all orders over 50 dollars. Expedited shipping delivers within 2 business days for a flat fee of 12 dollars. International shipping is available to most countries and typically takes 7 to 14 business days, though customs processing may add additional time. Customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes charged by the destination country.

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number. You can track your package at any time from the "My Orders" section of your account. If your tracking information has not updated for more than 5 business days, please reach out to our support team so we can open an investigation with the carrier.

## Account and Password Help

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it yourself from the account settings page by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in screen. A reset link will be emailed to the address on file and remains valid for one hour. For security reasons, our support agents can never see or tell you your existing password; they can only trigger a reset. If you are locked out of the email address associated with your account, please contact support with proof of identity so we can help you recover access.

## Warranty

Most Acme Gadgets products come with a limited one-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects under normal use. The warranty does not cover accidental damage, water damage, or normal wear and tear. To make a warranty claim, contact support with your order number and a description of the problem, along with photos if possible. Approved claims are resolved with a repair, a replacement, or a refund at our discretion.

## Contacting Support

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time. You can reach us by email at help@acmegadgets.example or through the live chat widget on our website. Premium members receive priority support and free next-day delivery on all eligible orders. We aim to respond to every inquiry within one business day.
