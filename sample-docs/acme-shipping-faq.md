# Acme Gadgets — Shipping FAQ

## How long will my order take to arrive?

Delivery time depends on the shipping method you choose at checkout. Standard shipping arrives in 3 to 5 business days within the continental United States. Expedited shipping arrives within 2 business days. International orders typically take 7 to 14 business days, not counting any time the package spends in customs. Business days are Monday through Friday and exclude public holidays.

## How much does shipping cost?

Standard shipping is free on all orders over 50 dollars; below that it is a flat 4 dollars 99 cents. Expedited shipping is a flat 12 dollars regardless of order size. International shipping is calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight. Premium members receive free next-day delivery on all eligible orders within the continental United States.

## Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Acme Gadgets ships to most countries worldwide. Please note that the customer is responsible for any import duties, customs fees, or taxes charged by the destination country. These charges are collected by the carrier or local customs office and are not included in the price you pay at checkout.

## When will my order ship?

Orders placed before 2 PM Eastern Time on a business day are usually processed and handed to the carrier the same day. Orders placed after that cutoff, or on weekends and holidays, ship on the next business day. During major sale events processing may take an extra day; we will always email you when your order is on its way.

## How do I track my package?

As soon as your order ships you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link. You can also track any order from the "My Orders" section of your account. Tracking information can take up to 24 hours to show its first update after the carrier scans the package.

## My tracking has not updated in several days. What should I do?

Carriers occasionally go quiet for a day or two, especially over weekends or during bad weather. If your tracking has not updated for more than 5 business days, contact our support team and we will open an investigation with the carrier on your behalf.

## Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

If your order has not yet shipped, contact support as soon as possible and we will do our best to update the address. Once an order has shipped we can no longer change the destination, but you may be able to reroute the package directly with the carrier using your tracking number.

## Do you ship to PO boxes or military (APO/FPO) addresses?

Standard shipping can be delivered to most PO boxes and to APO/FPO military addresses. Expedited and next-day options are not available for these destinations because they rely on carriers that do not service them.

## What happens if my package is lost or arrives damaged?

If your tracking shows delivered but you cannot find the package, first check with neighbors and around your property, then contact us within 7 days so we can file a claim. If an item arrives damaged, contact support within 48 hours of delivery with photos and we will arrange a free replacement, including return shipping.

## Which carriers do you use?

Domestic orders ship via a mix of national carriers depending on your location and the speed you selected. International orders are handed off to a local postal partner once they reach the destination country. You do not choose the specific carrier, but the tracking number we send works across the whole journey.
