# Acme SoundPods Pro — User Manual

## What's in the box

Your SoundPods Pro package contains the two earbuds, the charging case, a USB-C charging cable, and three pairs of silicone ear tips in small, medium, and large. The medium tips come pre-installed on the earbuds. A quick-start card is also included.

## Charging the earbuds and case

Connect the USB-C cable to the port on the back of the charging case and to any USB power source. A full charge of the case takes about 90 minutes. The earbuds charge automatically whenever they are seated in the case. A full charge gives up to 6 hours of listening time from the earbuds, and the case holds roughly four additional full charges, for about 24 hours of total playback.

The light on the front of the case shows its status: solid green means fully charged, pulsing green means charging, and amber means the case battery is low and should be recharged soon.

## Pairing with your phone or computer

The first time you open the charging case, the earbuds enter pairing mode automatically and the case light blinks white. On your device, open the Bluetooth settings, wait for "SoundPods Pro" to appear in the list of available devices, and select it. Once connected, the case light stops blinking.

To pair with a different device later, place both earbuds in the case, leave the lid open, and press and hold the small button on the back of the case for about 5 seconds until the light blinks white again. The earbuds are now discoverable by the new device.

## Touch controls

Each earbud has a touch-sensitive surface on the outer face:

- Play or pause audio: tap twice on either earbud.
- Skip to the next track: tap three times on the right earbud.
- Go to the previous track: tap three times on the left earbud.
- Answer or end a call: tap twice on either earbud during a call.
- Activate your phone's voice assistant: press and hold either earbud for 2 seconds.

A short tone confirms each action. Touch controls can be customized in the Acme Audio companion app.

## Water and sweat resistance

The SoundPods Pro earbuds carry an IPX4 rating, which means they resist splashes and sweat from any direction. They are suitable for workouts and light rain. They are not waterproof: do not submerge them, wear them while swimming, or rinse them under running water. The charging case has no water resistance rating and must be kept dry.

## Updating the firmware

Firmware updates improve sound quality and fix issues. Open the Acme Audio app with the earbuds connected; if an update is available the app will prompt you. Keep both earbuds in the open case near your phone during the update, which takes a few minutes. Do not close the case or move far away until it completes.

## Troubleshooting

**Only one earbud plays sound.** Place both earbuds back in the case for 10 seconds, then remove them. If the problem continues, reset the earbuds (see below).

**The earbuds will not connect.** Make sure they are charged, then remove "SoundPods Pro" from your device's Bluetooth list and pair again from scratch.

**Resetting the earbuds.** Put both earbuds in the case, leave the lid open, and hold the case button for about 15 seconds until the light flashes amber three times. This clears all pairings and returns the earbuds to factory settings; you will need to pair them again.

## Specifications

- Driver size: 11 millimeters
- Bluetooth version: 5.3, range up to about 10 meters (30 feet) in open space
- Earbud battery life: up to 6 hours per charge
- Total battery life with case: up to 24 hours
- Water resistance: IPX4 (earbuds only)
- Charging port: USB-C
- Weight: 5 grams per earbud, 45 grams for the case
